Welcome!

Thanks for stopping by! We need your help to locate Elephants, livestock, humans and more in a challenging set of aerial images. You can contribute by drawing tight rectangles around each individual object you encounter! Also, you are not alone: an AI model in the background constantly learns from you and tries to find relevant objects in all the unlabeled images.
